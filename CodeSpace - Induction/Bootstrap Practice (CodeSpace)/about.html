About Us

About Us

Here, at Sam's 'Cute-as-Pie Canines', we love our dogs like they're our world, because they are! In 1946, after the marked end of the 2nd World War, we came about many lost dogs as remnants of military canines. These dogs were fit and healthy (and, obviously quite well trained, too!), but were in need of homes. This sparked our interest in dog-rehoming, and we got to it right away.

-

After years of trial and error within our business, mainly due to reactive dogs from the war, we have been taught many lessons pertaining to this line of work, trained up many puppies of all breeds and personalities, and have therefore perfected our abilities in the field. We pride ourselves on our progress, and what we've accomplished, and can say whole-heartedly:

WE ARE THE BEST!!
